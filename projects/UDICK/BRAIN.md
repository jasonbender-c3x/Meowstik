## ⚡ What Is This Project?

UDICK is the central orchestration hub for reverse engineering, custom firmware development, and cross-environment integration of Carlinkit/T-Box hardware. It bridges the gap between high-level architectural planning and low-level firmware analysis.

**One-liner:** *"Universal orchestration for deep hardware reverse engineering and environment synthesis."*

**Key differentiator:** *Integrates NSA-grade reverse engineering tools (Ghidra) with a multi-device 'Fleet Command' orchestration layer.*
