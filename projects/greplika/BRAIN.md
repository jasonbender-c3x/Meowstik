## ⚡ What Is This Project?

Greplika is the companion-product branch of Meowstik: a persistent, device-present AI relationship product that can follow the user across phone, desktop, watch, vehicle, telepresence, and eventually embodied hardware.

**One-liner:** *"A local-first companion platform with continuity, memory, and real-world device presence."*

**Key differentiator:** *Built on the same agent, tool, and memory stack as Meowstik, but optimized for emotional continuity and cross-device presence rather than software workspaces.*

## 💼 Business / Goal

Greplika targets users who want more than a chatbot: they want a persistent companion with memory, personality continuity, and presence on the devices they already carry. The business opportunity is a premium companion product built on a local-first agent stack, with optional subscriptions for premium memory, voice, device presence, and specialized providers.

Primary market:
- users dissatisfied with shallow companion apps
- users who want continuity across devices
- technically curious early adopters willing to self-host or run local-first stacks

Monetization candidates:
- subscription for companion features and continuity
- premium voice / presence packs
- hardware and telepresence integrations
- optional provider add-ons rather than a single-model lock-in
