## ⚡ What Is This Project?

CatPilot is the in-vehicle and driving-agent branch: it collects the setup docs, FrogPilot references, and car-facing UX ideas needed to put Meowstik-style agent behavior into a car console or driver-assistance workflow.

**One-liner:** *"Vehicle-agent experiments spanning FrogPilot, setup docs, and car-console presence."*

**Key differentiator:** *Bridges software-agent UX with real car surfaces instead of treating driving context as an afterthought.*

## 💼 Business / Goal

CatPilot is strategic infrastructure more than a standalone SKU today. It explores how a persistent agent can become useful in a vehicle context: setup help, project continuity in the car, telepresence-lite interaction, and eventually deeper integration with openpilot/FrogPilot-adjacent workflows.
