# CatPilot

## Scope

CatPilot is the vehicle-facing project space for car-console agent experiments, FrogPilot references, and the operational notes required to bring Meowstik-style presence into driving-adjacent environments.

## Why it matters

If Greplika is the persistent companion and Greplit is the builder workspace, CatPilot is the bridge into the car: a place where assistant continuity, voice interaction, and open driving-tool ecosystems can meet real hardware and safety constraints.
